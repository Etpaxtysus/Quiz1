Submarine Monument

The Surabaya Submarine Monument, or Monumen Kapal Selam Surabaya, is a unique attraction in Surabaya, Indonesia. It features a decommissioned Russian submarine, the KRI Pasopati 410, transformed into a museum that offers an immersive experience into the life of submariners.

Located in the heart of Surabaya, this historical site is a must-visit for those interested in Indonesia's maritime heritage and history, providing a memorable glimpse into the challenges faced by the crew during its service in the Indonesian Navy while allowing visitors to explore the vibrant city and culture of East Java.
