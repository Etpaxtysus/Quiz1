Local Food in Surabaya

Rawon Surabaya

One of the delicious local foods in Surabaya, Indonesia is "Rawon." Rawon is a flavorful and aromatic black beef soup that is not only a culinary delight but also a cultural symbol of the city. This dish is known for its rich, dark color, which is achieved by using a special blend of ingredients, including keluak nuts, which give it its distinctive black hue.

Rawon is typically served with steamed rice, bean sprouts, lime wedges, and a sambal (spicy chili paste) on the side. It's customary to squeeze fresh lime juice into the soup to enhance its tangy flavor. The combination of the tender beef, aromatic broth, and the bold flavors of the spices make Rawon a beloved and iconic dish in Surabaya, enjoyed by locals and visitors alike.
